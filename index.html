Welcome to
Karachi

Karachi, city and capital of Sindh province, southern Pakistan. 
It is the country’s largest city and principal seaport 
and is a major commercial and industrial centre.

The city has been variously called Caranjee, Crochey, Krotchey, 
Currachee, and Kurrachee, all of which are believed to be variants 
of the same name. In the 18th century it was known as Kalachi-jo-Goth, 
meaning “the village of Kalachi”.

Kamran Khan Tessori

(Governor Sindh of Karachi)


The Governor's House, officially known as Sindh Governor House, and also known by its former names including Government House, Governor-General's House and President's House, is a palace in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. It is the official residence of the governor of Sindh. The current governor of Sindh is Kamran Tessori.

Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a prominent Pakistani politician, former cricketer, and the founder of the political party Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI). He served as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan from 2018 to 2022.

Nawaz Sharif

Nawaz Sharif is a prominent Pakistani politician and the leader of the Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N). He served as the Prime Minister of Pakistan three times and is known for his focus on economic development.

Shehbaz Sharif

Shehbaz Sharif is a Pakistani politician and the current Prime Minister of Pakistan. He is the brother of Nawaz Sharif and has previously served as the Chief Minister of Punjab, focusing on infrastructure and development projects.

What's Good in KARACHI

Karachi is Pakistan's largest city and a vibrant hub of culture, commerce, and history. Known for its bustling markets, beautiful beaches, delicious food, and rich cultural heritage, Karachi is a city that offers something for everyone. From the lively streets of Saddar to the serene views of Clifton, Karachi is a blend of modernity and tradition, making it a city worth exploring.
